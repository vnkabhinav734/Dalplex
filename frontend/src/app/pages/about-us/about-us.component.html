About Us

DalSports is a student-run organization at Dalhousie University that provides opportunities for students to participate in a variety of sports and recreational activities.

Our mission is to promote a healthy and active lifestyle among students by providing access to affordable and inclusive sports programs.

We offer a range of programs and events throughout the year, including intramural leagues, drop-in sessions, and tournaments. We also work closely with other student groups and organizations to promote physical activity and wellness on campus.

If you have any questions or feedback, please don't hesitate to contact us.
